Frequently Asked Questions - CloudSync Pro

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.

Q: Can I share files with people outside my organization?
A: Yes! You can create secure share links with optional password protection and expiration dates. External users don't need a CloudSync account to access shared files.

Q: What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
A: You'll receive email notifications at 80%, 90%, and 100% of your storage quota. Once you reach 100%, you won't be able to upload new files until you free up space or upgrade your plan. Existing files remain accessible.

Q: How long are deleted files retained?
A: Deleted files are moved to the trash and retained for 30 days. During this period, you can restore them. After 30 days, they're permanently deleted. Enterprise customers can customize retention policies.

Q: Is there a file size limit for uploads?
A: Individual file size limit is 50GB for web uploads and unlimited for desktop sync clients. We recommend using the desktop client for files larger than 5GB for better performance.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

Q: Can I export all my data?
A: Yes, you can request a complete data export from Account Settings > Data Export. We'll prepare a downloadable archive within 48 hours.

Q: What's the difference between sync and backup?
A: Sync keeps files identical across devices in real-time. Backup creates point-in-time snapshots that are retained according to your version history settings.

Q: Do you offer phone support?
A: Enterprise plan includes 24/7 phone support. Business plan customers have email support with 4-hour response time during business hours.

Q: How secure is my data?
A: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We offer zero-knowledge encryption as an add-on, meaning only you can decrypt your files. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
